History

ERamen shop is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. It was established since 1910 in Japan and brought to Indonesia in 2015. It was first established in Tokyo by Master Miyamura and his family. With a small kitchen and a handful of wooden tables, it was the birthplace of what would later become a legendary ramen.
